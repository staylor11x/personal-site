That was until Microsoft dramatically reduced their token limits :/ As such I am being forced to write this section with my own two fingers.

In my spare time I enjoy building cool things, like this website, and other personal project, check out my GitHub for more information on that front. Hopefully you find this site somewhat interesting, it's supposed to be over-styled with a slightly deranged tech-stack... who else have you seen with a C++ backend?
